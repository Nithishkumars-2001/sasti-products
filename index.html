How to Use ABC Malt

Simple instructions to get the most out of your SASI ABC Malt

To use ABC malt powder, simply mix it with either warm water or milk. A common method is to add 1-2 teaspoons of the powder to 1 cup of warm milk and mix well. It can also be taken raw such as mixed with hot water.

1.

Measure the Powder
Take 1-2 teaspoons (5-10g) of SASI ABC Malt powder.

2.

Add to Liquid
Add the powder to a cup of warm milk or water.

3.

Mix Well
Stir thoroughly until the powder is completely dissolved.

4.

Enjoy
Consume immediately for best results. Can be taken daily in the morning or evening.
